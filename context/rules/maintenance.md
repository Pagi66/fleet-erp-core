MAINTENANCE RULES
RULE M-001: Planned Maintenance Enforcement
All PMS defined tasks shall:
Be auto generated based on schedule
Be assigned to responsible personnel
Require completion tracking
RULE M-002: Maintenance Completion Validation
A maintenance task shall only be marked complete if:
All required steps are executed
It is verified by the responsible authority
RULE M-003: Overdue Maintenance Handling
If a maintenance task is not completed within its scheduled time:
It shall be marked overdue
It shall be re planned
Notification shall be sent to supervising authority
RULE M-004: Organizational Maintenance Execution
All tasks within ship capability shall:
Be executed by ship staff
Be supervised by department heads
RULE M-005: Intermediate Maintenance Escalation
If maintenance exceeds ship capability:
A Maintenance Request must be generated
Task must be transferred to FSG
Status must be tracked until resolution
RULE M-006: Dockyard Maintenance Control
Major repairs and overhauls shall:
Be scheduled under Annual Maintenance Plan
Be controlled by Log Comd
Require formal approval before execution
RULE M-007: Repairable Item Preservation
All repairable items shall:
Be repaired
Be returned to supply chain
Not be discarded
