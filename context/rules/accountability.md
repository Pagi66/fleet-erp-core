ACCOUNTABILITY RULES
RULE A-001: Departmental Responsibility
Each department head shall:
Maintain all required records
Ensure operational readiness
RULE A-002: Documentation Control
All manuals, drawings, and PMS documents shall:
Be accounted for
Be updated
Missing items logged
RULE A-003: Supersession Control
During role change:
Full inspection must be conducted
All records must be transferred
Supersession certificate must be completed
RULE A-004: Negligence Detection
If defects are discovered post handover:
Investigation must be triggered
Responsibility must be assigned
RULE A-005: Machinery Readiness
MEO shall ensure:
All machinery is fit for operation
Personnel are trained
