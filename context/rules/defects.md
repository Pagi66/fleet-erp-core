DEFECT AND ESCALATION RULES
RULE D-001: Defect Recognition
A defect includes:
Equipment failure
Performance abnormality
Safety risk
Material degradation
RULE D-002: Defect Recording
All defects shall:
Be logged immediately
Be recorded in relevant registers
Include detailed description
RULE D-003: Delayed Repair Tracking
If repair cannot be completed:
It must be recorded in Delayed Repair Record
Reason must be documented
RULE D-004: Escalation Threshold
If ETTR exceeds 21 days:
Defect shall be escalated
Converted to major repair workflow
RULE D-005: Immediate Escalation
Critical defects affecting safety or availability shall:
Be escalated immediately
Be routed through MCC
RULE D-006: FSG Escalation
If FSG cannot resolve defect:
Case must be escalated to Log Comd
Decision must be enforced
RULE D-007: Return to Port Decision
If defect cannot be handled at sea:
MEO must report to CO
CO must decide operational action
